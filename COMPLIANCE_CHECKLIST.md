# Compliance Readiness Checklist (High-Level)

This is not legal advice. It is a scoping checklist to support discussion with qualified counsel.

## Regulatory Classification & Licensing
- Confirm central and state-level legal position for online gaming in India.
- Determine if your games are classified as real-money or non-monetary.
- Validate licensing requirements (if any) and permitted jurisdictions.

## KYC / AML
- Identity verification (name, address, age) and age-gating.
- AML monitoring for unusual activity and transaction thresholds.
- Ongoing verification for high-risk users.

## Responsible Gaming Controls
- Self-exclusion and time-outs.
- Spend limits, loss limits, and session reminders.
- Responsible gaming messaging and easy access to help.

## Payments & Taxation
- Confirm tax treatment for your model (GST and reporting).
- Payment gateway onboarding aligned to gaming policies.
- Segregation of operational funds vs. player balances.

## Game Integrity
- Fairness testing (RNG certification if applicable).
- Anti-collusion and anti-bot mechanisms.
- Dispute resolution process and clear rules.

## Data Protection & Security
- Data minimization and retention policy.
- Encryption in transit and at rest.
- Audit logs and breach response plan.

## Platform Governance
- Grievance officer and takedown workflows.
- Advertising and affiliate compliance.
- Ongoing compliance reporting and monitoring.
